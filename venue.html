Ground control

A world-class venue in the Latin Quarter.

The Centre International de Conférences de Sorbonne Université is one of Paris's premier conference facilities, located in the historic 5th arrondissement — steps from the Seine, the Panthéon, and the Jardin des Plantes.

The venue features a main auditorium, multiple breakout rooms for community tracks and workshops, and open spaces designed for networking and collaboration.
