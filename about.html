About the conference

The conference for everyone who works with data.

Compute! is built on a conviction: that understanding the tools and systems behind your data is what keeps decisions your own.

Our conviction

The hard part is comprehension.

For people who refuse to treat their tools as black boxes — who want to understand, not just use. We believe that clarity about algorithms and data pipelines is what separates informed decisions from lucky ones. You drive the tools. Not the other way around.

From the community

From the people who maintain the stack.

The organizing team has contributed to Jupyter, Scikit-learn, Apache Arrow, Mamba, and many of the tools that power modern data science. We built the conference we wanted to attend.

Crew manifest
